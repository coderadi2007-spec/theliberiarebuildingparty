Volunteer With The REBUILDERS

Your skills — social, technical, cultural, logistical — are the fuel of the national rebuilding. Choose your lane below and register through the Contact page.

SM

Social Media Sharing

Amplify the REBUILDERS message across Facebook, WhatsApp, X and TikTok.

D2D

Door-to-Door Canvassing

Meet neighbors, share the 7 Cardinal Pillars, and register new members in your district.

TR

Translation

Translate speeches, flyers and radio spots into Kpelle, Bassa, Mano, Vai, Grebo and more.

LO

Logistics

Coordinate transport, venues and materials for county rallies and mobilization events.

TX

Tech Support

Contribute to the Rebuilders Clouds System, ERP dashboards and county data pipelines.

FM

Field Mobilization

Organize community meetings, palava-hut dialogues and campus outreach.

MC

Media & Content

Photography, video, podcast and design work for the Rebuilders Media Center.

SO

Security & Order

Support marshalling and safety at rallies, meetings and official party events.
