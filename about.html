About The REBUILDERS

Born from a divine burden for the nation, the Liberia Rebuilding Party is a movement of citizens, professionals and youth committed to a total national transformation.

Visionary Leader & Founder
Who We Are

A National Movement, Not a Political Career.

Founded on May 30, 2023 and certified by the National Election Commission, The REBUILDERS is a citizens-first party rooted in Integrity, Transparency, Accountability, Innovation, Inclusivity, Sustainability and Patriotism.

We do not exist to trade favors; we exist to rebuild the foundation. Our headquarters at 24th Street / Russell Avenue, Sinkor, Monrovia is the operations center of a nationwide mobilization.

Our Mission

Rebuilding the Foundation, Empowering the People.

Our mission is to unite Liberians across tribe, faith and region under the banner of a Smart and Green Liberia — powered by transparent governance, an industrial economy and world-class human capital.

We commit to a decade of disciplined execution across the 7 Cardinal Pillars — measurable, cloud-tracked and open to the public.
