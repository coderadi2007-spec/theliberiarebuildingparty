National Leadership

The men and women driving the REBUILDERS mission — from the Visionary Founder to the National Executive Council.

Meet Our Leaders

Servants of the Nation

Legal minds, technologists, mobilizers and community leaders — one team, one mission.

Visionary Leader & Founder

Cllr. Luther N. Yorfee

Founder and visionary of The REBUILDERS, guiding the movement to restore Integrity, Unity and Prosperity to Liberia.

Chairman, National Executive Council

Cllr. Enoch Garlawolu

Leads the day-to-day governance of the party and chairs the executive body driving the rollout of the Rebuilders Clouds System across every county.

Chairman, National Governing Council

Atty. Juvenal C. Pearson, Sr.

Provides legal and strategic stewardship of the party constitution and the long-term governance framework of The REBUILDERS.

National Secretary General

Mr. Azariah M.W. Golay

Coordinates the national secretariat, membership mobilization and the regional command structure from Monrovia to every county.

Pioneering First Chairman

Cllr. Lavela Walker

Established the initial structures of the party and led the certification process with the National Election Commission (NEC).

First National Secretary General

Hon. Felix C. Nimley

Instrumental in mobilizing the early membership and building the grassroots foundation of the REBUILDERS movement.

County Leadership

Leaders Across the Counties

Chairmen, chairladies and youth vanguard leaders driving the REBUILDERS movement in every county.

Montserrado County Chairman

Saah Roberts

Leads the REBUILDERS movement across Montserrado County, mobilizing supporters and coordinating grassroots activities in Liberia's most populous county.

Youth Vanguard Chair, Grand Gedeh

H. Junior Gosah

Chairs the Youth Vanguard in Grand Gedeh County, engaging young people in the party's vision for a Smart and Green Liberia.

Chairman, Grand Gedeh County

J. B Onaldo Hinneh

Serves as County Chairman for Grand Gedeh, leading the local executive and organizing REBUILDERS activities across the county.

County Chair Lady, Grand Gedeh

Esther Toe Wesseh

Champions women's participation and community mobilization as County Chair Lady for Grand Gedeh, strengthening the party's grassroots base.
